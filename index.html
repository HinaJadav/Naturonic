Some common questions
were often asked

How long will it take to get my order?

It depends on where you are. Orders processed here will take 5-7 business days to arrive. Overseas deliveries can take anywhere from 7-16 days. Delivery details will be provided in your confirmation email.

Can I return my product?

We always aim for make sure our customers love our products, but if you do need to return an order, we’re happy to help. Just email us directly and we’ll take you through the process.

Are the products tested for their skin-friendliness?

All formulas are dermatologically tested for their skin-friendliness.

Are the Naturonic products sustainable?

For Naturonic, health and quality are inseparably bound to a responsible approach to nature, sustainable development and social responsibility. Naturonic relies on renewable materials such as natural cork, as well as recyclable bottles and tubes. Secondary packaging is avoided as much as possible. An intelligent refill system also protects the earth’s natural resources..

What are the benefits of Naturonic oils?

Face and body essential oil blends – packed with omega-rich oils, vitamins and antioxidants, and being highly compatible with skin – add moisture.

How do I select my skin type?

We define the different categories of skin type as:
1. Dry: Rough, peels easily.
2. Balanced: Not too dry, not too oily.
3. Oily: Heightened sebum (face oil) production.
4.Combination: Dry and balanced in some areas, but oily in others.
